[LIST=1]
[*]S-OFF and Root
[*]Unroot
[*]Install 4ext recovery app to flash it
[*]Boot menu: Reboot, Hot reboot, Reboot recovery, Reboot to fastboot, Reboot to hboot, Power off

[*]Run an ADB/Fastboot Command Prompt
[*]Install Busybox via an app
[/LIST]
